Submission Process

Important ! Every Ph.D student at Le2i must submit a paper at least once during his thesis (in 2nd or 3rd year).

Papers in PDF format must be submitted by mail here.


Revision Process

Each participant at the Doctoral Day will be assigned at least one paper to review.

The members of the Committee will assign the reviewer for each paper. They will as well review supplementary materials.

The Committee strives for a double blind review.

A best paper will be chosen by the committee after final submission.


Registration

Registration deadline is the 1st February 2019.

Participants need to register by mail here before this date.
